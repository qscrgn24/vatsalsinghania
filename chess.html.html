Early career as a chess player

I started playing chess when I was 6 years old. My school at the time had just started a chess coaching program in the free period and my elder brother had enrolled for it. He becam very interested in the game and started calling a private coach in our home. I remember just going and sitting right next to him whenever his class was there and I got very interested in the game. My parents asked me if I also wanted to learn and then I started training with my elder brother as well.
